I don't hate you.

Resent you.
Or envy you.
For throwing me away as you grew.
Either way Im too prideful.
Too bold. Too grounded.
To allow myself to not go down the chute.
See, I don’t need that.
But want that. I can see that.
Even though it’s only the surface.
What even was the purpose?
For letting me go.
Regardless
I promise I’ll never hate you.
I’ll just hate myself.
For not doing this to you a long time ago.

Do you ever think?

what if this is an illusion? What if we’re all just walking around with the same splattered thoughts in the back of our heads? Like are we really even friends? And what am I doing here? And when is this going to end? And Is it just me or are you too sculpting your face into the whole smile and pretend? Or do you ever think what if being content is being lazy and being happy is being crazy and being sad is accepting the fact that you haven’t made any changes lately? If that’s the case, then how could you ever possibly win? Because see now I’m confused as to where to even begin. And when you lay your head down at night are you too thinking where have I even been? I couldn’t even tell you. I’m just going with the motions. It seems like every day is a masterpiece of chaos and miscommunicated emotions. And unfortunately each day we wake up and wet our broken brushes. But did you ever think what if we just stopped painting these ugly commotions?

The Pit.

I can see myself crumbling, falling down gracefully. Emotionless with no desire to grab on to something. Outstretched arms with more than enough capability to lift me back up, but nothing seems more appealing to me than that empty pit. It’s tireless. To let go and try less. To neglect life’s responsibilities and succumb to my insecurities as they encompass me and swallow me whole. Into that black hole. That lifeless pit that wants me.
